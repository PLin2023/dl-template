Our Foundry

Southeast Foundry was established in 1995 and has since grown significantly. The foundry now spans 30,000 square meters, with an annual casting capacity of 1,200 tons and a valve and fitting production capacity of 400,000 pieces.

In 2020, the company was recognized with a "Regional R&D Center" award. Our foundry employs advanced sand casting and precision investment casting techniques to produce various stainless steel valves, pipe fittings, and hardware. We are capable of manufacturing products in a range of materials, including WCB, CF8, CF8M, and various other stainless steel grades.

At Southeast, our philosophy is to provide customers with the best service while delivering high-quality products. We have established a comprehensive quality inspection system, supported by state-of-the-art equipment such as CNC machining centers, spectrometers, ultrasonic flaw detectors, and magnetic particle testing devices. These tools ensure that quality is rigorously controlled at every stage of production.
